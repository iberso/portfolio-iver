“KeRing” Kelas Daring (Online Class)

Mobile App Development

I created an android application that was planned to be similar to Google Classroom, but due to a short deadline, only a few features were successfully created.

Additional features of this application:
  • teachers can post announcements.
  • students will get notified when the teacher posts a new announcement.
  • the teacher can see students who have not seen the announcement and send notification to them.
